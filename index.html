IntoTech Conference

The IntoTech conference took place on the 11.11.2017 at the ImpactHub in Vienna and was organised by myself and a friend over the course of around a year in our free time. It was a free conference for people interested in technology, but with no or very little experience, who are interested in getting started with tech.
