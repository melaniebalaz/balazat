Challenges and patterns

Hi! I am Melanie, an interdisciplinary scientist/developer/artist, with a passion for technology, bioinformatics and various media of art and design. Originally from Vienna, Austria, I am currently living in Helsinki, Finland and completing my masters degree in Life Science Informatics at the University of Helsinki. I like challenges of all sorts (in my freetime you will find me climbing up big rocks) and learning new things and I have come a long way from completing my diploma degree in multimedia in Vienna to working as a data analyst in cancer research in Helsinki. I write code and poetry, I love finding patterns in everything around me, and I like to tell stories through illustration, photography, video editing, and design. Inbetween I like to organise events, such as the IntoTech Conference, or the River Jump! festival. I am most happy outside and one of my personal challenges is how to combine this love for movement, travel and nature with science and technology.
