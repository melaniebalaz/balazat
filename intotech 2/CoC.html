Code of Conduct

What we expect of you.

IntoTech is about getting people into technology, regardless of age, race, gender, sexual orientation, political ideology or past. Please help us to make everyone feel welcome and treated with respect.

Leave controversial symbols, political signs and such at home and celebrate that we all enjoy creating new things for the betterment of humanity.

When interacting with other participants, please keep in mind that some of our audience may not be accustomed to large crowds, may not see things the way you do, or may simply not be interested in talking to you. Please observe personal space and be nice to each other.

If you, for any reason, feel that you have not been treated according to these standards, please contact any of our staff in the IntoTech t-shirts on the conference grounds, or call the organizers.
