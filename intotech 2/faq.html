Is the location wheelchair accessible?
The ImpactHub is accessible with a wheelchair.

Why ImpactHub?
ImpactHub is one of the innovation spaces in Vienna, where technology meets all sorts of other disciplines. There are always people around, and there is a strong focus on community.

My kid would like to go to IntoTech, are they too young?
IntoTech does not have any age limits, however all the talks and workshops will be in English, so in order for your kid to enjoy it they should be quite good at English. Please do accompany your child to IntoTech if they are very young.
